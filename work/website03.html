Project

This client is a subsidiary of a gaming company, responsible for promoting the sales of their games. In this project, the client required us to renovate several existing pages of their career site, including the top page. The client wanted the website to be simpler and shorter than the previous version to make it easier to convey information to potential candidates.

Visual Design

In this project, I specifically focused on creating a layout that allows users to view an entire page with minimal scrolling while making it easy for them to understand the information.
For example, I arranged the subpage links as boxes with pictures on the top page. This way, users can see all the content on one screen while also getting an idea of what kind of content to expect on the subpages from the images. For the main visual, I used a carousel layout to show potential candidates the company's atmosphere through multiple pictures displayed on one screen.
