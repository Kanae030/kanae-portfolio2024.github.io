Project

The client company is involved in clinical trials. This project involves renewing their recruitment site, which has become outdated. The company is now actively recruiting, including individuals without experience as Clinical Research Coordinators (CRC). However, many potential candidates are unfamiliar with the CRC role, leading to a shortage of applicants. To address this, the new recruitment site will provide detailed information about the CRC position, allowing people to learn about the role from the basics to the finer details. Our team will focus on creating comprehensive content to educate potential candidates about the CRC job.

Visual Design

The CRC role requires high accuracy, serious responsibility, and perseverance to develop new medicines. Therefore, the client company seeks candidates with these qualities. Additionally, the client wants candidates to understand that being a CRC is a rewarding occupation, allowing them to contribute to society by supporting the creation of new medicines. As a result, our team decided to make the website look dignified.
My role in this project was to design the UI for several subpages (11 pages). I ensured that the UI design followed the tone and mood of the top page.
