Project

The client company is a clinical trial company. This project is renewing the client's recruitment site because the previous recruitment site has gotten old and they started to recruit actively. Their recruitment problem is that they are also recruiting people who do not have experience as a clinical research coordinator (CRC), however many people do not know CRC, and the client company could not get many candidates. So they want to tell people what the CRC job is like through the new recruitment site. Our team decided to mainly make content that people can learn from the basics to the details of the CRC job.

Visual Design

CRC is required high accuracy, serious responsibility and perseverance to make new medicines, so the client company looks for the candidates who have such personality. Adding to that, the client wants the candidates to know that CRC is a rewarding occupation that they can contribute to society by supporting the creation of new medicines. Therefore our team decided to make the website look dignified.
