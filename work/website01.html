Project

The client specializes in software development, providing services to individual clients and public institutions. The scope of this project was to refresh the look of the client's website and their need to express that their company is more appealing than competition. In a thorough research we have concluded that the strength of the company is its CEO. He, being a seasoned software engineer, is aware of the impact of the work environment on the employees. This became a base for the concept of renewal and we chose the concept to be “Engineers are great (エンジニアは偉大だ)”, something the CEO himself mentions in the interview.

Visual Design

In the interview with the client we have discovered that the main reason candidates join their company is a friendly and cheerful atmosphere. The client wanted candidates visiting the website to feel the company’s friendly culture. This led me to utilize colors and shape of circles present in the company’s logo to convey client’s identity and environment. I chose blue as the primary color to highlight the cleverness of the software engineers, complementing it with images of the employees to impress visitors.
