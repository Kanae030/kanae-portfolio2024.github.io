Project

The client company is an IT company that creates software for other companies and public institutions. This project is renewing the client's recruitment site. The client wanted the candidates to know the company's attractiveness through the recruitment site. So our team searched for their attractiveness and the differences from other companies, and we found some attractive points. And we defined their most attractive point as the CEO. He is also a software engineer so he knows what is the best environment for the employees well and tries to give it to the employees. Therefore we decided the website’s concept and the main copy is “Engineers are great (エンジニアは偉大だ)” that the CEO mentioned in his interview.

Visual Design

The client told us the main reason why most of the candidates decided to join the company is that the employees are friendly and the company’s atmosphere is cheerful, and the client wanted the candidates to feel the friendly company culture through the website. Therefore I used the color and shape of circles of the company's logos to make the website have the company identity, and friendly and cheerful atmosphere. And also, I chose blue as the main color to impress the users the smartness of SE. Adding to that, I used many pictures of employees on the top page to convey the users the company's atmosphere.
