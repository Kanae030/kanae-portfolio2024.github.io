Visual Design

This logo was created for “issue 100” which is a marketing service. Three designers, including myself, proposed designs for the logo, and ultimately, this design was selected by the customer. The service represented by this logo identifies 100 social issues that consumers are concerned about, enabling companies to highlight their social value by advertising how their business addresses these issues.
I designed this logo to convey that companies can gather extensive consumer feedback on social issues through this service. The design represents society with a cityscape, where the buildings are created as speech bubbles to signify that the feedback pertains to social issues. The use of multiple colors emphasizes the diversity of voices and opinions that users can access.
